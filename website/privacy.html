Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Effective date: 2026-07-06

NotchFlow is developed in the EU (notchflow.eu). We minimize data collection by design.

What we collect

  • License validation: license key and device name sent to LemonSqueezy when you activate premium.
  • Updates: Sparkle checks appcast metadata from notchflow.eu.

Clipboard monitoring (opt-in)

Clipboard history is disabled by default. When you enable it in Settings → Privacy, NotchFlow stores copied text and URLs locally on your Mac in Application Support. Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly copy it elsewhere. You can disable monitoring at any time.

Local API (opt-in)

The Raycast integration uses a local HTTP server bound to 127.0.0.1 only. A bearer token is stored in your Keychain. No external service receives API traffic.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics or telemetry in v1.0
  • No browsing history, file contents, or media library metadata uploaded to our servers
  • No clipboard data uploaded to our servers
  • No advertising identifiers

Your rights

Under GDPR you may request access, correction, or deletion of purchase records via the email used at checkout. Contact: privacy@notchflow.eu
